part3 部分

回答技巧

方法一：分类讨论

friend

  • *What do you do with your friends？*
  • *How do you make friends？*
  • *Do you have many friends？*
  • *Do you like chatting with friends online？（口语题目网上都可以找得到）*

问题1 ：你和朋友一起做什么？

回答1：Sometimes,我们去唱歌，and other times, if … then 我们去逛街+原因：因为逛街让我们放松（chill out）

问题4：你喜欢和朋友在网上聊天吗？

回答4：（先给一个肯定的回答）我不喜欢和朋友在网上聊天，＋原因：因为我觉得不真实，再＋做法：我更喜欢和朋友面对面聊天（face to face）。比如（for example），我经常在周末和朋友在cafe咖啡厅聊天

如果觉得太短就举例子！

方法二：WHY类

原因类的一般考官会问你诸如：“你认为科学课重要吗？为什么？”或者2018年3月part two 中“你想去的国家”这个topic下“why you want to live or work there”等等。

这一类题目，首要是分层次回答，比如：

  • first of all, I want to live there because那里有便利的交通
  • also，那里的东西很便宜cheap
  • most importantly, 那里的工资很高

可用如下词语替换，但最好有三条原因：

  • one of the reason is that it makes …展开性的
  • firstly
  • for one
  • and most importantly 扩展性
  • and also partly because it can
  • in addition
  • what’s more

自己整理原因，用于part one 考官问你”why you choose this subject/job”

原因（好处）：

  1. 带给我许多convenience
  2. 影响工作效率（work efficiency）
  3. 给我满足感fulfillment
  4. quite 便宜, easy to get
  5. that the reason why I
  6. 可以远离，get away from the noisy city life

方法三：个人喜好二选一

  • do you prefer A or B？
  • do you like A or B ？
  • which do you think is better A or B？
  1. 例：你喜欢住flat or a house
  2. 你喜欢在家吃还是在外面吃
  3. 好处：cheaper and safe —out expensive

回答：well, between the two, I would lean towards A because…好处and…, you know I feel that B is 不好的地方，so I am gonna go with A.

方法四：替换掉like

如何替换掉do you like? 喜欢：

  • I am fond of
  • I enjoy
  • I am really into
  • I am passionate about
  • I am a big fan

方法五：对于DO YOU THINK…类的题目

用两面答法

  • do you think children should play video games?

yea, I think. 孩子打游戏是有益的（先肯定回答）…because it can 促进孩子的团队能力（promote their team work skills）…, although it might have a negative influence on sth(尽管会影响他们的学习studies)，I still feel that…我仍然认为这是好的。

常用句式 ：

好处：

  1. give people an edge 交朋友 找工作 when they are looking for a job
  2. a necessary skill in the modern life
  3. practical(实用的)/ resourceful/ considerate/ open minded/ charismatic(有魅力的)
  4. promote their team work skills
  5. 吃鸡 give people an edge to make more friends

坏处 ：

  1. it takes a lot of time and efforts会占用很多时间和精力
  2. 有一个坏的影响on study

同义替换

  • 我认为:

    I feel that

    in my opinion

    the way I see it

常用idiom和句式套路

  1. 关于时间
    • kill time
    • time off 休假，need to take some time off
  2. 一些常用词组
  3. 经常一开口不知道说什么，开头语：
    1. the point I’d like to begin with is that
    2. I guess直接加句子
  4. 句式多样性，分句型总结

常用连接词

因果

…because

…so…

As a result, … 常用于part3

让步

Even if … , …即使

​ Even if you take a taxi, you’ll still miss the train.

…as long as … 只要

​ My parents don’t really care what job I do, as long as I’m happy.

转折

But…

However, …

补充

Besides, …

​ I don’t really want to go. Besides, it’s too late now.

… also …

修饰

定语从句

… that …

… who …

… , which …

对比

On the other hand, …

​ My job is boring. On the other hand, it pays well.

… By contrast …（part3可用）

​ The coastal areas have mild winters. By contrast, the central areas are extremely cold in winter.

举例

like…

​ I’m into fantasy novels like Harry Potter and The Chronicles of Narnia.

For example, …

其他

in term of…

​ Larry’s last job was better in terms of salary.

instead of / rather than

​ Support was offered by the government rather than private companies.

​ We can deal with this chapter now instead of waiting until tomorrow.

不是连词却胜似连词

basically

actually

get

​ Basically, the car is in good condition, but the paintwork needs a bit of attention.

​ Harrison is actually very helpful.

​ I’ll get this paper finished by tonight.
